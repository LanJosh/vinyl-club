Vinyl Club

Hi.
Welcome to the Vinyl Club website.
We are a group of music lovers and vinyl enthusiasts who like to get together, hang out, and work on projects. Members enjoy being able to meet other like-minded people, learn about new music, and come to our social events. Our group also likes to promote local record stores and musicians. If you'd like to learn more about us, meeting are held the second and fourth Tuesday of each month at 7:30 pm at 107 E Norwich Ave, Apt F.
We'd like to thank some persons for helping the club. Thank you to Svetoslav Simov at Fontfabric and Tyler Finck for letting us use their fonts on our website. Check them out at Fontfabric.com and tylerfinck.com for downloads of their word. Thank you to Christian H., Samipyylampi, and Thomas Heylen for the pictures; definitely check our their flickr pages to see more. And thanks for everyone else who is keeping vinyl alive.

Membership

Any person enrolled at the Ohio State University may become a member of Vinyl Club. There is no membership fee involved, but some social events may involve costs to partipate. Contact lan.74@osu.edu to be added to the roster, or come to a meeting and speak with any officer to join. Meetings are held the second and fourth Tuesday of each month at 7:30 pm at 107 E Norwich Ave, Apt. F
Membership gets you access to all of our meetings and information about any Vinyl Club events.
